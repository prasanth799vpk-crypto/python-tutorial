
Generative AI : It is a type of artificial intelligence that can create new content such as Text , Images , Audio , Video , 3D models.

Traditional AI (Analytical): Looks at a photo and says "This is a cat."

Generative AI (Creative): Looks at millions of photos of cats and then creates a brand new photo of a cat that has never existed before.

Examples of Generative AI : ChatGPT (text), Midjourney/DALL-E (images), GitHub Copilot (code), Sora (video).


🤖 1. Generative AI ante enti?

Definition (Simple):
Generative AI ante kotha content create cheyyagal AI
(text, images, code, videos…)

👉 Example:

nuvvu: “Leave letter raay”
AI: full letter generate chesthundi

👉 Real life example:

Photoshop lo image edit chesthav
kani Generative AI image ni scratch nundi create chesthundi

📌 Examples:

ChatGPT → text generate
DALL·E → images generate
🧠 2. LLM (Large Language Model)

Definition:
LLM ante pedda text data meeda train ayina AI model
idi manam matlaadite ardham chesukoni reply ivvagaladu

👉 Simple ga:
LLM = Brain of ChatGPT

👉 Real-world example:

Nuvvu friend tho chat chesthav
friend answer istadu knowledge base meeda

LLM kuda alane:
👉 internet lo unna huge data ni nerchukuni reply istundi

📌 Examples:

GPT (ChatGPT)
Gemini
Claude
📚 3. RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

Definition:
RAG ante:
👉 AI answer ivvadam mundu
👉 external data (files, DB, docs) nundi info teesukuni
👉 accurate answer ivvadam

👉 Simple ga:
RAG = Google search + ChatGPT combo

🔥 Real-world example:

👉 Without RAG:

Nuvvu: “maa company leave policy enti?”
AI: guess chesthundi (wrong avvachu)

👉 With RAG:

AI → company document open chesthundi
akkada nundi exact info teesukuni answer ivvadi

👉 Use case:

Company chatbot
Customer support bot
🤖 4. AI Agent

Definition:
AI Agent ante task ni complete cheyadaniki actions teesukune AI

👉 Simple ga:
Agent = AI + Thinking + Action

🔥 Real-world example:

👉 Nuvvu:
“Book movie tickets for tonight”

Agent:

movie search chesthundi
timings check chesthundi
seats select chesthundi
booking complete chesthundi

👉 Idi just answer ivvadam kaadu
👉 panulu kuda chesthundi

⚡ 5. Agentic AI

Definition:
Agentic AI ante self-thinking + decision making AI system

👉 Simple ga:
👉 “emi cheyyalo AI ne decide chesukuntundi”

🔥 Real-world example:

👉 Goal: “Plan my trip to Goa”

Agentic AI:

flights search
hotels compare
itinerary create
budget calculate

👉 nuvvu step-by-step cheppalsina avasaram ledu

🧩 6. AI Agents vs Agentic AI
Concept	Meaning
AI Agent	specific task complete chesthundi
Agentic AI	multiple tasks plan chesi autonomously work chesthundi
